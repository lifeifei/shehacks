HOW IT WORKS...
A hassle-free and transparent way to split the costs of an online purchase.
Paykitty enables you to purchase online items as a group. Groups can be made up of friends, family, community groups, and even strangers!
Add an online item you'd like to purchase as a group
Create your group and invite your friends
Paykitty collects the money from your group and purchases the item
Your item is delivered to your nominated address
